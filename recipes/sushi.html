Sushi

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tuna
  • Avocado
  • Celery
  • Edible seaweed (Nori)

    • Steps

    1. Boil rice
    2. Cut tuna, avocado, and celery into small slices
    3. Open edible seaweed (Nori) & layer rice, avocado, celery, and tuna (in written order)
    4. Role together
    5. Cut into prefer slice size
    6. Eat and Enjoy!
