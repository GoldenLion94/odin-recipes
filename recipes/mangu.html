Mangu

Ingredients

  • Green Plantain
  • White Cheese
  • Salami
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

    • Steps

    1. Boil plantain 45min
    2. Mash plantain & mix with water, butter, olive oil, and salt
    3. Fry salami, cheese, and egg
    4. Marinate white onions in apple cider vinegar and olive with salt for 20 minutes
    5. Saute marinated white onions for 5-10 minutes (depending on how crunchy you like your onions)
    6. Serve & Enjoy!
